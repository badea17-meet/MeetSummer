Problem

Car accidents is a very crucial problem nowadays. and one of the biggest causes of car accidents is driving under the influence of alcohol. When you are drunk, your confidence level is higher than usual and you are not aware of the alcohol effects on your discretion. As a result, you may make the wrong decision and drive while you are drunk.

Vision

We envision a society where roads are safe day and night. By providing awareness and alternatives to those over the legal alcohol limit, we encourage drivers to make the right decision before putting the keys into the ignition.

Our vision is to decrease the amount of car accidents caused by drunk drivers as well as saving lives. We are willing to increase awareness of the consequences and grievous effects of driving while drunk

Solution

Approaching people coming out of parties or bars and offering a breathalyzer in order to test the alcohol percentage in their blood and check if they are eligible to drive a car. If not the volunteers will recommend alternatives for driving such as : suggest nearby restaurants or nearby hotels.

How it Works

Working with volunteers, the volunteers will approach people coming out of parties or bars and offer them a breathalyzer in order to test the alcohol percentage in their blood and check if they are eligible to drive a car.

The breathalyzer we are using is called BACtrack.
The breathalyzer is connected to your smartphone. The device checks the amount of alcohol in the blood and sends the information to the smartphone.

After that information is sent to the phone, our application calculates the time needed in order for the percentage of alcohol in your blood to be below the legal limit. According to these results the app suggests nearby places as alternatives to visit such as restaurants, coffee shops, motels, hotels and also gives the possibility of calling a taxi.
The volunteers we are working with are friendly passionate young people who are willing to make a change in their society
